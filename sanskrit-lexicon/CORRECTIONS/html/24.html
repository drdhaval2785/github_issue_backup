The governing principal for assigning 'H1'...'H4' to elements is drawn from the Preface to MW (see http://www.sanskrit-lexicon.uni-koeln.de/scans/csldoc/dictionaries/prefaces/mwpref/mwpref11.html):

![image](https://cloud.githubusercontent.com/assets/6393033/4817243/eee91e06-5ee6-11e4-94ed-3a2d4ee4901e.png)

Thus the only the fact about the headword tta that is used in assigning it 'H1' is that the headword is in Devanagari type; the fact that there is a leading '-' before the IAST form of this headword is not used in deciding which H-code to assign.
