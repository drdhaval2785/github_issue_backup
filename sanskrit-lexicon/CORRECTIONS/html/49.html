funderburkjim :

This issue is devoted corrections to headwords from the PWG dictionary.

The starting list contains 90 headwords and was obtained from AllvsMW.txt in
[SanskritSpellCheck](https://github.com/drdhaval2785/SanskritSpellCheck/) as all lines matching the
regular expression:
```
:PWG$
```
Also the words don't contain 'rxx' - form as their reason for being in the AllvsMW.txt list.
Since the list is short no further reductions were deemed necessary (there are a few duplicates)

After listing headword corrections a list of the remaining headwords (those not requiring correction) from this list will be shown.
