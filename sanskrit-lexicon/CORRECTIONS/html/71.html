Title - MW72 corrections from faultfinder with fuzzy suggestions Part 1

funderburkjim :

This begins examination of 230 headwords from MW72 (1872 edition of Monier-WIlliams Sanskrit-English) dictionary. These are chosen from sanhw1.txt as words that:
1. Occur only in mw72
2. Have some letter sequence not found in MW (this via faultfinder3a)

The examination of these cases is aided by producing [suggestion words](https://funderburkjim.github.io/mw72-only-fuzzyalpha.txt) from sanhw1 that are 'nearly the same' as the questionable word and if possible result in an alphabetical ordering at the position of the headword in the list of all mw72 headwords.

This part1 deals with the first 120 of the 230 cases to be examined.
