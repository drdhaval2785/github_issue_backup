@sanskritisampada Regarding case 201. There are some special *tricks* to keep in mind when you
edit issues in Github. One trick regards angle brackets. In 201 you entered:
```
ss : Previous word wrong entry Correct entry
```
BUT notice that when you look at the comment the two words in angle brackets have vanished!
There are various ways to emphasize words in a comment which you could use instead of
angle brackets. For instance you could italicize the words by putting an asterisk before and after the
word or you could make the words bold by putting two asterisks before and after the word. For
instance by typing your comment as:
```
ss : Previous word wrong entry **nimiktanEmiktika** Correct entry **nimittanEmittika**
```
It would display as

ss : Previous word wrong entry **nimiktanEmiktika** Correct entry **nimittanEmittika**
