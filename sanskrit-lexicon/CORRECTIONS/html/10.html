As background:
Regarding why the 'titO' errors occur. In Thomas's digitizations Thomas represents Devanagari words using the HK transliteration. This HK transliteration represents the two diphthongs
```
slp1 HK
O au
E ai
```
Thus there is no way for the usual HK to represent an a-u or a-i hiatus.

I have gotten around this for one or two dictionaries by 'adding a 0' in the HK:
```
slp1 HK-extended
au au0
ai ai0
```
