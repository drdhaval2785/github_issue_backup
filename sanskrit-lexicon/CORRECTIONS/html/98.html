and spelling patterns are not of the 'rxx' variety.

There may be a small number of typos.

another means.

Is this slightly different form ok? better? worse?

and the [vei-only-notrxx-page.txt](https://gist.github.com/funderburkjim/b4bb0614925edff2fb76) used by fuzzyalpha.py. sanhw1.txt (in this corrections repository) is also an input to fuzzyalpha.py.
